Who we are

Robinsons maintenance department started by providing aftercare and support to our installation division in the 1980’s. Over the years the department has developed into a business in its own right and is now known as Robinsons Facilities Services.

Robinsons Facilities Services now assist over 500 sites with planned and preventative maintenance for boilers, air conditioning, electrical and renewable technologies, offering 24/7/365 day reactive cover for all of its contracted clients which include care homes and hotels to industrial units, factories and offices of varying sizes.

By offering a personal, professional and comprehensive service Robinsons Facilities Services has become a market leader within the Yorkshire Region. Click here if you’d like to contact us to discuss your requirements further.
